Auto Tok — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Overview

Auto Tok is a personal desktop application for automated video publishing. This Privacy Policy describes what information is accessed, how it is used, and how it is protected.

2. Information We Access

The Application accesses the following information solely to provide its core functionality:

  • OAuth Access Tokens: Tokens issued by YouTube (Google) and TikTok to authorize video publishing on your behalf.
  • Video Content: Video files and metadata you provide to the Application for publishing.
  • Platform Account Information: Basic account identifiers returned by platform APIs (e.g., channel ID, user open_id) necessary to complete publishing operations.

3. How Information Is Used

Information accessed by the Application is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate with YouTube and TikTok on your behalf.
  • Upload and publish video content to your connected accounts.

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the above.

4. Data Storage

  • OAuth tokens are stored locally on the user's machine in encrypted or restricted-access files.
  • No personal data, tokens, or video content is transmitted to or stored on external servers controlled by this Application.
  • Video content remains under your control at all times.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Data is transmitted only to the platform APIs (YouTube, TikTok) as required to fulfill publishing operations, subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application integrates with:

  • Google / YouTube Privacy Policy
  • TikTok Privacy Policy

Your use of those platforms is governed by their respective policies.

7. Data Retention

Locally stored tokens are retained until you revoke access through the respective platform (YouTube or TikTok) or manually delete them from your machine. The Application does not maintain any server-side data retention.

8. Your Rights

You may revoke the Application's access to your accounts at any time through the account settings of the respective platform:

  • YouTube: Google Account Permissions
  • TikTok: TikTok Settings → Privacy → Manage App Permissions

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
